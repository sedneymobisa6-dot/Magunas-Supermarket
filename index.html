Our Story

From Hawker to
Household Name.

Simon Wachira started hawking in Mukuyu market in the 1980s — saving every shilling. In 1990 he opened a small retail shop. Today Magunas Supermarket has 35 branches across Kenya, competing head-to-head with Naivas, Quickmart and Carrefour.

1980s
Simon Wachira starts as a village hawker in Mukuyu market, Murang'a. Saving every shilling with a dream.
1990
First retail shop opens at Kwa Maji area — now famously known as Magunas Stage.
2000s
Maguna Andu Wholesalers launched. Goods sourced directly from manufacturers. The name means "Beneficial to the People."
2012
Nokras Hotel founded — a four-star facility in Murang'a, diversifying into hospitality.
Today
35+ branches across Kenya — Nairobi, Mt Kenya, Embu, Meru, Nyeri, Kirinyaga, Kitui and growing.
